About Kerala

Kerala, affectionately known as "God's Own Country," is a tropical paradise nestled on the southwestern coast of India. This enchanting state offers a diverse tapestry of experiences, from serene backwaters and pristine beaches to misty hill stations and vibrant wildlife sanctuaries.

With its rich cultural heritage, ayurvedic traditions, and warm hospitality, Kerala promises an unforgettable journey for every traveler.

Discover the Beauty of Kerala

Top Tourist Attractions

Alleppey Backwaters

Experience the tranquil beauty of Kerala's backwaters on a traditional houseboat. Glide through serene waterways, observe local life, and enjoy breathtaking sunsets.

Munnar Tea Plantations

Explore the rolling hills covered in lush green tea plantations. Enjoy panoramic views, visit tea factories, and trek through picturesque landscapes.

Fort Kochi

Discover the rich history and colonial architecture of this charming coastal town. Explore Chinese fishing nets, spice markets, and vibrant street art.

Rich Cultural Heritage

Kathakali and More

Kerala's cultural landscape is as diverse as its natural beauty. Experience the vibrant colors and intricate storytelling of Kathakali, a classical dance-drama. Witness the strength and agility of Kalaripayattu, one of the oldest martial art forms in the world.

Immerse yourself in the rhythmic beats of Chenda melam during temple festivals, or enjoy the soothing strains of Carnatic music. Kerala's festivals, like Onam and Thrissur Pooram, offer a spectacular display of traditions and community spirit.

Delectable Cuisine

A Culinary Paradise

Kerala's cuisine is a tantalizing blend of flavors, aromas, and textures. Savor the delicate spices in a traditional Kerala fish curry, or indulge in the crispy goodness of appam with stew. Don't miss the opportunity to try:

  • Malabar Biryani
  • Sadya (a grand vegetarian feast)
  • Puttu and Kadala Curry
  • Kerala Prawn Curry
  • Pazham Pori (banana fritters)

Complete your culinary journey with a cup of aromatic filter coffee or refreshing coconut water straight from the shell.
